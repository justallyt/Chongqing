About CCPIT

Founded in 1983, CCPIT is a foreign trade and investment promoting agency located in Chongqing, which includes major responsibilities as follows: implementing national strategies and Chongqing municipal plans in order to boost foreign trade, bilateral investment as well as economic and technological cooperation; establishing business cooperation mechanism with counterparts overseas; receiving overseas economic and trade delegations and organizing Chinese delegations to visit foreign countries; holding or organizing Chinese companies to attend economic and trade exhibitions, forums, fairs and related international conferences; representing industry and commerce in the field of foreign trade and involving into the formulation of trade and economic policies and rules, foreign trade negotiation and making of international commercial rules; carrying out legal consultations, commercial mediation, trade and maritime arbitration; signing and issuing certificates of origin for export products and other certificates and documents related to foreign trade, providing intellectual property services such as patent application, trademark registration, litigation and litigation rights safeguarding; organizing industries and enterprises to cope with trade disputes; providing trade and economic information, training services, etc.
